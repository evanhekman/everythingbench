# SEVEN WONDERS CARD LIST
Format:
Card Name (card_id) - Color | Cost | Benefit | Chains | Copies
- "Card Name" is the literal name of the card, card_id is what's used to refer to it
- "Color" is the color of the card (brown, gray, blue, red, green, yellow, purple)
- "Benefit" is a description of what the card does.
- "Chains" is whether the card chains to or from anything else
- "Copies" is how many copies of the card will be available across all hands.
